ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm an aspring artist living in the United States. I picked up digital art as a hobby for the first time in 2016, and ever since, I've been dabbling in it whenever I can. I use the software Adobe Photoshop and own a Wacom Intuos Draw tablet.

Besides drawing, some of my other hobbies include piano, flute, and web development. In fact, this website is a personal project on its own! I also enjoy writing and spending time with my family, friends, and pets.
